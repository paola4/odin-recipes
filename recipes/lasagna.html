Lasagna

Lasagna is made by layering noodles between a creamy cheese sauce, tomato sauce, and a mince of some kind. The mince can be vegetarian or you could use beef.

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • tomato sauce
  • cheese

Steps

  1. First, cook your vegetables in a pot.
  2. Cook your lasagna noodles in boiling water.
  3. Assemble your lasagna.
